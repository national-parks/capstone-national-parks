Meet Sue Bradford

Purpose: This project will be a National Parks website with the focus on obtaining up to date relevant information such as activities and amenities available regarding the nations National Parks. It will also be used to plan personal travel and to be aware of any upcoming volunteer activities.

Audience: This site is designed with a frequent national parks visitor and volunteer audience in mind. It will be targeted to families or individuals looking to explore the nations National parks and all the amenities and services the national parks have to offer. Targeting audience to include: individuals who enjoy the outdoors, vacation planning families, and vacation or tour planning companies.

Goal: The primary goal is for the targeted audience to view and interact with the site to learn about the nations National Parks and all the amenities and activities they have to offer. A secondary goal is to showcase the great outdoors with all its natural magnificence, wildlife, and beautiful scenery and landscape.


Persona:

Name: Sue Bradford

Age: 55

Profession: Recently retired from the corporate world where she was a mid-level manager for a fortune 500 company in the computer software industry.

Technology: Windows 10 user. Uses a Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon laptop. iPhone 11 Pro Max. Canon EOS-1D X Mark III DSLR camera. Celestron Nexstar 8SE Computerized Telescope.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Sue is a people person with great communication and personable skills. Sue is very active on social media with friends, family, and business and personal interests. Sue is very punctual with her time in the corporate world. When Sue needs a break from the city corporate world she enjoys the great outdoors, hiking, biking, and exploring whenever she gets the chance. Sue volunteers regularly with local charities and with the National or State parks when time permits. When looking for vacationing or volunteering opportunities she wants to get as much information as possible in the shortest amount of time. Sue is an avid amateur photographer and astronomy star gazer.

Frustrations and Needs: Sue gets frustrated when volunteering information isn't updated regularly or the information is out dated. Sue needs a reliable site to get to the most relevant information. Sue also does not like to plan her outdoor vacation or voluntary time when all the relevant and important information is not readily available and updated frequently. When considering a National Park visit, Sue is frustrated when she learns at the last minute right before traveling, that an activity she was planning on has been postponed or is no longer available and the information has not been updated. Sue is always looking for her next outdoor adventure to explore and enjoy the great outdoors.

Goals: Sue's main goal is to volunteer her time and explore each and every National Park this great nation has to offer. Sue does not want to spend a lot of time confirming outdated information regarding activities offered or tracking down relevant detail and contact information regarding volunteering opportunities. Sue's goal is to have a site that is up to date with all relevant planning information all located together about an upcoming planned vacation or volunteering opportunity.

User Story: As a site user Sue needs to get all relevant information to better plan and prepare for her trip and be aware of all the possible amenities and activities available to enjoy the National Parks to the greatest extent possible.
