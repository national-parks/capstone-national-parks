Meet Sue Bradford

Purpose: This project will be a National Parks website with the focus on obtaining up to date relevant information such as activities and amenities available regarding the nations National Parks. It will also be used to plan personal travel and to be aware of any upcoming volunteer activities.

Audience: This site is designed with a frequent national parks visitor and volunteer audience in mind. It will be targeted to families or individuals looking to explore the nations National parks and all the amenities and services the national parks have to offer. Targeting audience to include: individuals who enjoy the outdoors, vacation planning families, and vacation or tour planning companies.

Goal: The primary goal is for the targeted audience to view and interact with the site to learn about the nations National Parks and all the amenities and activities they have to offer. A secondary goal is to showcase the great outdoors with all its magnificence, wildlife, and beautiful scenery and landscape.
